# Changelog - 2026-07-16

## [Added] Configurable Owner Revenue & Management Fee System

- Created 7 new models for a complete owner revenue and management fee system:
  - **`pms.charge.type`**: Configurable charge types (Rent, Cleaning Fee, Pet Fee, Damage Waiver, Resort Fee, Booking Fee, Cancellation Fee, Early Check-in, Late Checkout, Lodging Tax, Sales Tax, OTA Commission, Credit Card Fee, Owner Maintenance Expense, Discount, Refund, Security Deposit) with product mapping and classification flags (revenue, tax, expense, refund). Includes a seed method for default charge types.
  - **`pms.revenue.policy`**: Management fee policies supporting `percentage`, `fixed`, `percentage_plus_fixed`, and `tiered` (scaffolded) fee types with minimum/maximum fee caps and configurable rounding methods (`round`, `round_up`, `round_down`, `no_rounding`). Includes effective dates and active flag.
  - **`pms.revenue.policy.line`**: Per-charge-type allocation rules defining owner, manager, and liability share percentages, commission basis selection (`excluded`, `gross_amount`, `owner_allocated_amount`), and expense responsibility (`owner`, `manager`, `split`). Validates that shares total 100% for positive revenue lines. No negatives allowed.
  - **`pms.owner.agreement`**: Owner-property agreements with overlap prevention (no two active agreements can overlap for the same property and date range), settlement frequency, revenue policy reference, currency, analytic account support, and a calculation-preview method for sample scenarios.
  - **`pms.reservation.charge`**: Individual reservation financial components stored separately (not derived from reservation totals). Supports reversal linkage for refunds via `reversal_of` field. Integrates with Odoo taxes. Tracks source system and reference.
  - **`pms.owner.settlement`**: Settlement engine with state flow (`draft` → `calculated` → `reviewed` → `finalized` → `paid`, plus `cancelled` and `exception`). Calculates owner gross proceeds, commission base, management fee, owner expenses, credits, and net payout from reservation charges. Stores immutable JSON snapshots of agreement and policy data on finalization. Write-protected after finalization. Enters exception state for unmapped charge types or missing active agreements.
  - **`pms.owner.settlement.line`**: Per-charge settlement breakdown with allocation amounts (owner, manager, liability), commissionable amounts, management fee distribution, and human-readable calculation descriptions.

- **Key business logic implemented**:
  - Commission basis: `excluded` (zero), `gross_amount` (full line amount), or `owner_allocated_amount` (owner's share only).
  - Refunds and adjustments reverse the original allocation rules (owner, manager, liability, commission basis, and management fee).
  - Taxes remain separate liabilities and are never counted as owner or management-company revenue.
  - Cancellation fees treated as their own configurable charge type, not as rent.
  - Historical immutability: finalized settlements store JSON snapshots of agreement + policy data; changing policies or agreements does not affect finalized settlements.

- **Views created**:
  - List, form, and search views for all 7 new models.
  - Revenue & Fee Policy tab on the Owner Agreement form showing linked policy allocation rules.
  - Calculation preview interface on the Owner Agreement form.
  - Full menu integration under Properties and Owners menus, plus Configuration.

- **Security**: ACLs added to `ir.model.access.csv` for all 7 new models. `ir.sequence` records created for agreements and settlements.

- **Manifest**: Added `analytic` dependency, new view files, and sequence XML to `__manifest__.py`.
